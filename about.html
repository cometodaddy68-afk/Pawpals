About PawPals India 🐾

PawPals India is a zero-investment dropshipping store bringing premium pet products to pet parents across India. We believe every pet deserves the best — without breaking your budget.

Our Story

Started in 2026, PawPals was born from a simple observation: Indian pet parents struggle to find quality pet products at fair prices. Most brands charge too much, and the cheap alternatives don't last. We decided to change that by sourcing directly from manufacturers and passing the savings to you.
